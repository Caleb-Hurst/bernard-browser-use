
# How to Request Time Off

To request time off:

- Click **Request Time Off** and fill out the form inside the modal that pops up.
 To select a starting or ending date:
  1. Click inside the date field to open the calendar dropdown. You cannot type into the Starting and Ending fields but instead need to use a calendar modal to select a date
  2. You will not be able to submit the form unless you have filled out the Starting and Ending fields.


- To Approve PTO
1. Navigate to the EMPLOYEES tab and select the desired employee from the given list. This will bring you to the employee profile
2. From the employee profile find the PTO tab and approve or deny any PTO from there
